Bikes are fun. But why?

This website is a reminder to the world that riding bikes is fun.

Here is an incomplete list of things that are less fun than riding bikes:

  • Arguing about which bike has the best technology
  • Making fun of people who ride different bikes than you
  • Mocking trails that are different than your trails

I hope this list reminds everyone that bikes should be fun to ride

People should avoid arguments and making fun of people or trails
